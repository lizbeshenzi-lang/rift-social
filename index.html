The Story

Built for the Long Night

Rift Social opened its doors in 2019 with a simple idea: Nairobi deserved a space that felt like it was designed for Nairobi. Not imported from somewhere else. Not trying to be Shoreditch or SoHo. Ours.

Five floors up with the city spread below you, a bar programme built around East African botanicals and spirits, and a sound system that respects the music — Rift Social is the room you've been looking for.
